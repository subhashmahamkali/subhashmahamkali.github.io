## Timeline

- **Undergrad (Agriculture):** built strong foundations in crop science, field methods, and applied problem solving.
- **Master's training:** moved into molecular biology, lab protocols, and sequence-oriented analysis.
- **PhD at UNL:** integrated fieldwork, genomics, and high-performance computing pipelines.
- **Current direction:** computational biology and data-intensive roles with real-world impact.
